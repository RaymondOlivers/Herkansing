How to grow your food without digging

No wonder why a lot of people are not turning their gardens into productive vegetable gardens. Just the idea alone to get a spade and dig up the whole plot makes many a person too tired to even get off the couch. What if we told you that you can grow all the vegetables you can eat, without even putting a spade in the ground? If we have your interest, continue reading!

No dig gardening

The idea of no-dig gardening is very simple. All the seeds and plants are planted in a compost layer on top of the ground.
