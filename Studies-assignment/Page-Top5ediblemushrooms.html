Top 5 edible mushrooms

Did you know there are over 20.000 mushrooms? A lot of those are a real treat to the eye: bright colors, amazing shapes and some are even bioluminescent! Not all the mushrooms are edible, and we would like to start with a fair warning: do NOT pick if you are not a 100% sure about whether the mushroom is edible or not. Use multiple sources to confirm your findings: all in all, some of the mushrooms can kill you.

Having said that (and assuming you are still reading this and didn't got so scared that you clicked the page away), there are some that can be easily recognized. We gladly help you along to find these delicious treats.
